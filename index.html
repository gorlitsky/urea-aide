UreaAide™ Dosage Calculator

Dosing

Dosing of UreaAide Urea is based on the European Best Practice Guidelines:
Urea is doses at 0.25-0.5 grams per kilogram of body weight per day, usually in divided doses one to two times a day but can be taken up to four times a day.
Example: A 60 KG person would take 15-30 grams of Urea per day in divided doses. That is, 1 packet once or twice a day, 1 scoop once or twice a day, or six tablets once or twice a day. With tablets you may find it easier to take four tablets three times a day.
